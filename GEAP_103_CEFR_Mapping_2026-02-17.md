# GEAP 103: Basic Computer Skills — CEFR B1 Mapping (Verified)

**Course:** GEAP 103 Basic Computer Skills (Foundation Semester, B1)

**Source:** Council of Europe (2020), *Common European Framework of Reference for Languages: Learning, teaching, assessment — Companion volume*, Council of Europe Publishing, Strasbourg.

## Purpose

This document maps each GEAP 103 CLO to CEFR B1 descriptors that capture its language demands. GEAP 103 is a language course delivered through digital and AI contexts; the CEFR descriptors therefore target communicative competence, not technical mastery by itself.
